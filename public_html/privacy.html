Introduction

SaveMunch is a free app that helps to reduce food waste by connecting vendors to users. This app allow vendors to sell food approaching the “Best Buy Date” (NOT an expired food) at a cheaper price. On the other hand, customers can look for food items offered by stores near their location and get access to good-to-eat food at a discounted price. This app in no way is responsible for guaranteeing the quality of food offered or sold by the member store.
